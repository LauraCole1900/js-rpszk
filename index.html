Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock

Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock! The rules are as follows:

  1. Rock crushes Scissors and Lizard.
  2. Paper covers Rock and disproves Spock.
  3. Scissors cut Paper and decapitates Lizard.
  4. Lizard eats Paper and poisons Spock.
  5. Spock smashes Scissors and vaporizes Rock.
